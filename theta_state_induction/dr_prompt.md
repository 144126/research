# Theta State Induction — Deep Research Prompt

## Research Question
What are all known methods for deliberately inducing theta brainwave-dominant states (4–8 Hz), and what is the quality of evidence for each?

## Scope
- **Included:** Audio entrainment (binaural beats, isochronic tones, Hemi-Sync), breathwork (yoga nidra, holotropic, 4-7-8), sleep-transition exploitation (hypnagogia, WILD), devices (neurofeedback, CES, tACS, AVS, Muse), supplements (L-theanine, magnolia bark, ashwagandha), sensory deprivation (float tanks, dark rooms), contemplative practices (mindfulness, chi gong), practitioner systems (ThetaHealing, Monroe Institute), Internet communities (Reddit, TikTok), skeptical/critical perspectives
- **Excluded:** Basic theta neuroscience (covered in prior report ~/research/afformation_technique/report.md), clinical/therapeutic applications except where they inform induction methods, invasive brain stimulation (DBS), animal studies
- **Prior work:** ~/research/afformation_technique/report.md Finding 4 covers theta fundamentals; this report extends into induction methodology

## Key Deliverables
1. Comparison table of 20+ induction methods (reliability, speed, cost, ease, evidence grade)
2. Best Daily Protocol with 3 tiers (minimum, extended, enhanced)
3. Practitioner reports with explicit selection bias caveats
4. Skeptical assessment of placebo, replication crisis, commercial bias
5. Research gaps and future directions
6. 750+ cited references
