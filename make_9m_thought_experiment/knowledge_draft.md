### Key Insights

1. **Speed vs Safety Tradeoff**: Highest-return pathways (MEV, crypto arb) carry extreme regulatory/execution risk. Lowest-risk paths (bootstrap SaaS, consulting) take 5-10 years.

2. **Skill Overlap is Gold's Superpower**: The intersection of (SvelteKit + Rust + Python/ML + algo trading + Qdrant/vector) creates unique bundles — e.g., "edge AI trading platform" or "vector search for fintech" — that few competitors can build.

3. **Nigeria as Asymmetric Advantage**: Labor arbitrage (3-5x lower burn), market knowledge of African fintech, and rapidly digitizing economy ($40B by 2030) provide Gold with a home-field advantage.

4. **Dev Tool Acquisition Market is Hot**: Cloudflare ($85M for VoidZero), Vercel (Svelte team), and others are paying premiums for infrastructure/deep-tech talent. Building visible, high-quality OSS can lead to acquire-hire exits.

5. **Leverage Stack**: The optimal path likely combines 2-3 approaches — e.g., FTMO/arb for cashflow while building a SaaS; Nigerian fintech as primary with trading as hedge.
