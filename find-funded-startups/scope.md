# Phase 1: SCOPE — Research Framing

## Core Question
How to find recently funded startups fastest/easiest — tools, databases, strategies, automation, free/paid methods ranked by speed/cost/ease.

## 12 Dimensions of Investigation

1. **Technical/Mechanistic** — How funding databases work (Crunchbase collection pipeline, SEC EDGAR Form D, APIs, automation platform mechanics)
2. **Historical Context** — Evolution 2007-2026 (Crunchbase launch, Mattermark failure, industry consolidation)
3. **Current State-of-Art** — 2026 landscape of all startup funding databases, free alternatives, AI-powered signal detection
4. **Quantitative Evidence** — Pricing, record counts, update frequency, contact coverage, response rates, latency
5. **Stakeholder Analysis** — Database companies (Crunchbase, PitchBook, CB Insights), users (VCs, sales, freelancers), data sources
6. **Competing Approaches** — Crowd-sourced vs analyst-curated vs SEC-verified, paid vs free, manual vs automated
7. **Criticisms & Limitations** — Data staleness, missing rounds, expense, inbox flooding, coverage gaps
8. **Contrarian Perspectives** — Are funded startups actually better clients? Is Crunchbase overrated? Competition problem
9. **Future Trajectories** — AI making data free/real-time, API-ification, AI agent outreach
10. **Regulatory/Legal/Ethical** — SEC data rights, GDPR/CCPA, CAN-SPAM, ToS restrictions
11. **Geographic Variation** — US vs EU vs Asia tool strengths, coverage gaps
12. **Practical Applications** — Step-by-step workflows, case studies, pricing comparison table

## In Scope
- All startup funding databases (Crunchbase, PitchBook, CB Insights, VCBacked, Growth List, Fundraise Insider, Fundup AI, Seedtable, FundBat, Free Startup Funding, Wellfound, Dealroom, OpenVC, Teahose, Harmonic, Specter)
- Free vs paid tier analysis
- Step-by-step filtering workflows
- Contact data availability
- Automation tools (n8n, Databar, PhantomBuster, Zapier)
- API access and pricing
- Free methods (Google Alerts, SEC EDGAR, TechCrunch, VC portfolios, Twitter/X)
- Lead enrichment (Apollo, ZoomInfo, Lusha, Hunter)
- Freelancer-specific strategies
- Response rate data
- Regional coverage differences
- Pricing comparison

## Out of Scope
- Upwork/Fiverr/Toptal platforms
- Cold email/proposal writing
- VC investment strategy
- Grant funding (SBIR)
- Startup valuation
- Full CRM pipeline

## Timeframe
2024-2026, current (2026) pricing and features

## Geographic Focus
Global — US, EU, major startup hubs
