# Phase 1: SCOPE — Research Framing

## Research Question
What is the best name for a chess training app (Socratic AI coaching, pay-per-use, Nigerian market, voice coaching, warm cream/coral/dark design)?

## Core Components Decomposed
1. Competitive landscape of AI chess app names
2. Naming theory for AI-native products
3. Nigerian/West African cultural naming preferences
4. Chess-specific naming conventions
5. Domain/trademark availability
6. Evaluation framework for name candidates

## In Scope
- All major AI chess coach apps naming analysis
- AI-native app naming patterns (2023-2026)
- Chess brand naming patterns
- Nigerian market cultural analysis
- Domain availability for .com, .app, .ai, .co, .io
- USPTO/WIPO trademark conflicts (Class 9, 41)
- User perception from forums/social

## Out of Scope
- Logo design
- Full trademark legal search
- App store optimization
- Pricing strategy

# Phase 2: PLAN — Research Strategy

## Search Dimensions (8 angles)
1. AI chess coach apps — competitive names
2. AI-native app naming trends 2025-2026
3. Chess brand names and conventions
4. Nigerian consumer brand preferences
5. Naming psychology and linguistics
6. Domain availability
7. Trademark landscape (Class 9/41)
8. User perception data from forums/social

## Priority Order
1. Competitor analysis (foundational context)
2. Naming theory (framework for evaluation)
3. Nigerian cultural analysis (market-specific)
4. Domain/trademark (practical constraints)
5. User perception (validation)
6. Synthesis (name generation + scoring)
