# DEEP RESEARCH COMMISSION

## Research Question

What is the best name for a chess training app that uses Socratic AI coaching, pay-per-use pricing (Nigerian market via Paystack), real-time voice coaching (Gemini Live), and a warm cream/coral/dark premium design? The app currently ships with the working name "Beee Chess" but this is widely considered weak.

## Purpose & Context

The developer needs to choose a final brand name for a production chess training web app (SvelteKit + Cloudflare Workers). The app is already built and deployed at chess.apexlinks.org under the working name "Beee Chess." The developer needs research-backed naming recommendations before committing to a rebrand.

### Audience
- Primary: The developer (indie hacker, technical founder) — needs data-driven naming recommendations with competitive landscape analysis
- Secondary: Future users (Nigerian chess players, global chess learners, rated 800-2000 ELO) — the name must resonate with them
- Tone: Analytical, data-driven, practical (not theoretical)
- Complexity: Accessible to a technical founder making a business decision

### Decision Context
- The name will be used for: domain, app store listing, social handles, logo, branding
- A rebrand from "Beee Chess" is expected
- The decision is high-stakes: changing later would be costly
- Key considerations: memorability, pronounceability, domain availability (both .com and alternatives), trademark availability, cultural fit for Nigerian market, distinctiveness from competitors

## Research Scope

### In Scope
1. Competitive analysis — every major AI chess coach/training app name and how it's positioned
2. Naming patterns — what patterns work for AI-native apps (like Cursor, Lovable, v0, Replit, Linear)
3. Naming patterns — what patterns work for chess brands
4. Analysis of "Beee Chess" — why it's weak and what to avoid
5. Specific name recommendations (top 10 candidates) with reasoning
6. Domain availability assessment for top candidates
7. Trademark conflict assessment
8. Cultural fit for Nigerian/West African market

### Out of Scope

- Full trademark legal search (just flag obvious conflicts)
