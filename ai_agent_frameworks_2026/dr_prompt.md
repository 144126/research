# Research Commission: Best AI Agent Framework 2026

## Research Question
What is the best AI agent research framework in 2026 — meaning the optimal combination of architecture, production readiness, developer experience, community support, and total cost of ownership for building, orchestrating, and deploying AI agents?

## Audience
Engineering teams and technical decision-makers. Tone: technical, data-driven, honest about weaknesses. Include ELI5 explanations for every hard concept.

## Scope
14 frameworks: LangGraph/LangChain, CrewAI, AG2/AutoGen, OpenAI Agents SDK, Claude Agent SDK, Microsoft Agent Framework, Google ADK, Mastra, Agno, DSPy, LlamaIndex, Haystack, smolagents, Strands Agents. Out of scope: no-code builders, domain-specific, browser automation, coding-agent-only tools, research-only frameworks.

## 13 Investigation Dimensions
1. Technical/Mechanistic
2. Historical Context
3. Current State-of-Art (June 2026)
4. Quantitative Evidence
5. Stakeholder Analysis
6. Competing Approaches
7. Criticisms & Limitations
8. Contrarian Perspectives
9. Future Trajectories
10. Regulatory & Governance
11. Geographic Variation
12. Practical Case Studies
13. User Sentiment

## Output Requirements
- 270+ sources minimum
- index.md (full report), evidence.jsonl, sources.jsonl, run_manifest.json, dr_prompt.md
- Executive Summary 1000-1500 words
- 8 findings, 600-2000 words each
- All quality gates satisfied
