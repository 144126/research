# Phase 1: SCOPE - Best BEP-20 Wallet

## Research Question
What is the best BEP-20 wallet in 2026 across security, usability, features, cost, and user satisfaction?

## Core Components
1. BEP-20 token standard fundamentals and requirements
2. Wallet security models (self-custody, MPC, hardware, seed phrase)
3. Feature comparison across top wallets
4. User satisfaction and real-world experience
5. Cost analysis (fees, gas, hidden costs)
6. Geographic and platform availability
7. Regulatory considerations

## Stakeholders
- Retail crypto holders (new & experienced)
- DeFi power users (frequent swappers, yield farmers)
- Long-term holders (security-focused)
- Developers building on BNB Chain
- Cross-chain users (multi-chain portfolio)

## Scope In
- BEP-20 token wallets (hot, cold, mobile, desktop, browser)
- BNB Chain (BSC) ecosystem
- Non-custodial and custodial options
- Free and paid wallets
- 2024-2026 timeframe
- English and global market

## Scope Out
- Exchange-only wallets (e.g., Binance exchange wallet)
- BEP-2 token wallets (old Binance Chain standard)
- ERC-20 or other non-BEP-20 wallets
- NFT-specific wallets
- Enterprise/custodial solutions

## Success Criteria
- Identify top 5-7 wallets with pros/cons
- Rank by security, usability, cost, user satisfaction
- Provide actionable recommendations per user type
- 270+ sources triangulated

## Key Assumptions
- Self-custody is generally preferred by crypto natives
- BNB Chain remains a top-3 L1 by TVL and usage
- Gas fees remain under $0.50
