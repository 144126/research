## Research Question
What is the best BEP-20 wallet in 2026 across security, usability, features, cost, and user satisfaction?

## Audience & Purpose
Crypto users (retail holders, DeFi traders, long-term investors) deciding which wallet to use for BNB Chain BEP-20 tokens. Informs purchase decisions.

## Scope
In: BEP-20 wallets (hot, cold, mobile, desktop, browser), BNB Chain ecosystem, 2024-2026, global market
Out: Exchange-custodial wallets, BEP-2 tokens, ERC-20 only wallets, enterprise solutions

## Dimensions
1. Technical: BEP-20 standard requirements, EVM compatibility, wallet architecture
2. Historical: Evolution of BEP-20 wallets, major security incidents
3. State-of-art: 2026 landscape with latest models and features
4. Quantitative: Fees, user counts, ratings, security scores, audit results
5. Stakeholders: Retail, DeFi users, long-term holders, developers, cross-chain users
6. Competing approaches: Hot vs cold, seed phrase vs MPC, mobile vs hardware
7. Criticisms: Trust Wallet complaints, Bitget support issues, hardware wallet controversies
8. Contrarian: When hardware is overkill, when hot wallets are sufficient, MPC trade-offs
9. Future: Account abstraction (BEP-753), smart contract wallets, gasless transactions
10. Regulatory: KYC requirements, MiCA compliance, geographic restrictions
11. Geographic: US vs EU vs Asia wallet preferences, availability
12. Practical: Setup time, recovery process, daily usage friction
13. Public opinion: App store ratings, Trustpilot, Reddit sentiment, expert reviews
