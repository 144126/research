# Phase 2: PLAN - Research Strategy

## Primary Source Targets
- **Academic:** Design journals (Design Studies, Journal of Brand Management), psychology of visual perception papers, semiotics research
- **Industry:** Branding agency case studies (Pentagram, Wolff Olins, Lippincott), Logo Lounge reports
- **Books:** Paul Rand's "Design, Form and Chaos", Meggs' "History of Graphic Design", Michael Bierut's "How to Use Graphic Design"
- **Technical:** Adobe Illustrator/Affinity Designer vector workflows, variable font specs
- **News:** Brand New (UnderConsideration), Design Week, It's Nice That
- **Expert:** Interviews with top designers, AIGA conference talks
- **Primary:** Design briefs, agency case studies, original logo guidelines
- **Contrarian:** Blogs critiquing golden ratio hype, arguments against branding
