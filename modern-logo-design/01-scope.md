# Phase 1: SCOPE - Research Framing

## Research Question
What are the complete principles, techniques, philosophies, methods, and ideas for creating breathtaking, professional modern logos that immediately communicate intended messages and feelings effectively, are memorable, and visually stunning?

## Core Components Decomposed
1. **Foundational Principles:** Gestalt theory [how brains see wholes before parts], color theory, typography, composition, balance, proportion, hierarchy
2. **Psychology:** How shapes/colors/forms trigger emotions; semiotics [study of signs]; cognitive fluency; mere-exposure effect
3. **Process:** Research → brainstorming → sketching → digitizing → refinement → presentation
4. **Techniques:** Vector construction, responsive/adaptive logos, motion logos, variable branding
5. **Typography:** Custom type, kerning, letterform engineering, font psychology
6. **Color Science:** Meaning, palette construction, monochrome viability, cultural variance
7. **Composition/Geometry:** Golden ratio, Fibonacci, dynamic symmetry, grid systems
8. **Philosophy:** Minimalism vs maximalism, timelessness vs trendiness
9. **Case Studies:** Iconic logo deconstructions + failure postmortems
10. **Future:** AI impact, generative systems, automation threats

## Stakeholder Perspectives
- **Academic (primary reader):** Wants intellectual rigor, data-driven analysis, historical context
- **9-year-old child (secondary reader):** Needs constant inline bracketed simplifications of every hard term
- **Practicing designer:** Wants actionable techniques and workflows
- **Client/commissioner:** Wants evaluation criteria and quality assessment framework

## In Scope
- All 12 dimensions listed in commission
- 10+ iconic logo deconstructions
- 5+ failure postmortems
- Step-by-step professional workflow
- Cultural/geographic variation
- Contrarian perspectives

## Out of Scope
- Web/UI design (logos only)
- Fine art not related to logos
- Print production specifics
- Trademark law in specific jurisdictions
- Animation production techniques

## Success Criteria
- 216+ unique sources across 4+ types
- Every factual claim cited [N] immediately
- Constant inline bracketed simplifications for child reader
- 25,000-50,000+ words
- Complete bibliography with NO placeholders
- Evidence persisted in evidence.jsonl
- All 8 phases executed

## Assumptions to Validate
- Professional logo design follows teachable principles (not pure talent)
- Minimalism continues to dominate but faces growing backlash
- AI tools augment but don't replace human logo designers
- Golden ratio's importance may be overhyped
- Most logo "rules" have valid exceptions
