# DEEP RESEARCH COMMISSION

## Research Question
What are the fastest evidence-based methods to increase short-term memory and working memory capacity — ranked by speed of effect (minutes to hours to days to weeks) — and what protocols, dosages, durations, and combinations produce the most reliable improvements?

## Purpose & Context
This research aims to provide a definitive, evidence-ranked guide for anyone seeking to quickly improve their short-term/working memory. The audience includes students, professionals, older adults experiencing age-related decline, and generally curious individuals. The central question is: given limited time, which interventions work fastest?

The key tension is between interventions that work immediately (acute effects within minutes to hours: sleep optimization, acute exercise, chunking strategies, cognitive load reduction) versus those requiring weeks of consistent practice (dual n-back, mindfulness meditation, supplements). The research must clearly delineate the time-to-effect for each intervention category and identify which combinations synergize.
