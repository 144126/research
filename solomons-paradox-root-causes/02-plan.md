### Triangulation Strategy
- Cross-reference claims across independent labs
- Compare experimental (lab) vs. observational (field) evidence
- Compare self-report vs. behavioral measures
- Note where evidence is single-source

### Quality Gates
- 270+ sources, avg credibility >75/100
- 3+ sources per major claim
- All evidence classes represented
- Contradictory evidence explicitly documented
