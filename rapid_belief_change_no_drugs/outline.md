3. **Finding 1: Memory Reconsolidation Without Drugs — The Brain's Native Change Mechanism**
   - Coherence therapy evidence (Ecker, Toomey)
   - The reconsolidation triad achieved purely psychologically
   - Single-session transformation evidence
   - Comparison: drug-free vs drug-assisted reconsolidation

4. **Finding 2: CBT and Cognitive Methods — Schema Change Across Disorders**
   - Akers et al. (2025) meta-analysis: CBT schema change (g = -0.23)
   - Ezawa & Hollon (2023): cognitive restructuring (r = .35, d = 0.85)
   - Cuijpers et al. (2025, JAMA Psychiatry): 375 trials meta-analysis
   - Predictive coding framework for CBT
   - "Does CBT change core beliefs?" debate

5. **Finding 3: Behavioral and Experiential Methods — Learning Through Action**
   - Behavioral experiments and exposure therapy
   - Inhibitory learning vs reconsolidation debate
   - Somatic experiencing evidence (RCTs, d = 0.94-1.26)
   - Body psychotherapy meta-analytic evidence
   - Interoceptive exposure

6. **Finding 4: Third-Wave and Metacognitive Approaches — Changing Relationship to Thoughts**
   - ACT evidence meta-analyses
   - Metacognitive therapy (Wells): large effects on metacognitive beliefs
   - Mindfulness: DMN changes, neuroplasticity
   - Self-compassion interventions

7. **Finding 5: Experiential and Imaginal Methods — Empty Chair, Narrative, IFS**
   - Empty chair/Gestalt evidence
   - Narrative therapy evidence (d = 1.36 for depression)
   - Internal Family Systems emerging evidence
   - Hypnosis and self-hypnosis (Eason & Parris meta-analysis, d = medium-large)

8. **Finding 6: Drug-Free Non-Ordinary States — Breathwork and Meditation**
   - Holotropic breathwork evidence
   - Intensive meditation retreat effects (DMN, BDNF)
   - Non-dual practices and self-dissolution
   - The "plasticity window" without drugs

9. **Finding 7: Self-Directed Methods — Written, Verbal, Identity-Based**
   - Expressive writing evidence and limitations (d ~ 0.16 overall)
   - Growth mindset interventions (d = 0.14-0.32)
   - Affirmations: boundary conditions
   - Identity-based change (narrative identity, self-concept)

10. **Finding 8: NLP, EFT, and Controversial Methods — Evidence vs. Hype**
    - NLP: systematic reviews show insufficient evidence
    - EFT: 56 RCTs, large effect sizes
    - Hypnosis vs placebo debate
    - User-reported evidence from Reddit/HN
