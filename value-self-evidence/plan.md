# Phase 2: PLAN — Research Strategy

## Research Roadmap

### Primary Source Categories (targeting 270+ total)
1. **Academic Philosophy (target: 100 sources)** — SEP entries, PhilPapers surveys, journal articles, monographs
2. **Experimental Psychology (target: 60 sources)** — Kahneman, Tversky, Banaji, Asch, Milgram, Zimbardo
3. **Historical Primary Texts (target: 40 sources)** — Plato, Aristotle, Hume, Kant, Nietzsche, Moore, Mackie
4. **User-Reported / Personal (target: 40 sources)** — Reddit r/philosophy, Hacker News, forum discussions, social media
5. **Practitioner (target: 30 sources)** — Marketing thought leaders, educators, art critics

### Knowledge Dependency Map
1. FIRST: Understand the "self-evident value" thesis in its strongest form (Plato, Moore, value realism)
2. THEN: Understand the strongest critiques (disagreement, bias, cultural variation, development)
3. THEN: Understand middle-ground positions (response-dependence, ideal observer, constitutivism)
4. THEN: Explore empirical evidence (psychology experiments, neuroscience)
5. THEN: Gather user-reported and practitioner perspectives
6. FINALLY: Synthesize and evaluate
