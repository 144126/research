## Triangulation Strategy

- **Core claims**: 3+ independent sources required
- **Design patterns**: Validate against 2+ real implementations
- **Conversion data**: Cross-reference CRO studies with industry benchmarks
- **Technical patterns**: Verify against official documentation + community examples
- **Geographic diversity**: Search specifically for Nigerian/African UX patterns as supplement

## Quality Gates

1. **Gate 1**: 216+ sources with avg credibility > 70
2. **Gate 2**: Evidence.jsonl populated with per-claim entries
3. **Gate 3**: Every factual claim in report has [N] citation
4. **Gate 4**: ELI5 inline explanations for hard concepts
5. **Gate 5**: 4+ source types represented
6. **Gate 6**: No placeholders, no fabricated citations
7. **Gate 7**: Prose >= 80%
8. **Gate 8**: No vague attributions
9. **Gate 9**: Speculative content labeled
10. **Gate 10**: Executive summary >= 800 words
