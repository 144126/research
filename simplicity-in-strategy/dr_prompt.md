# Deep Research Prompt

## Research Question
Does simplicity win in business strategy? What is the empirical evidence, theoretical foundation, and practical track record of strategic simplicity vs. complexity — and what are the limits/costs of each?

## Audience & Purpose
A 54-year-old man with deep academic exposure, who wants to understand the principle deeply and also have it be understandable to a 9-year-old. Purpose: personal understanding.

## Scope
In: Empirical studies linking strategic simplicity to firm performance; case studies of simplicity-driven companies (Southwest, Capitec, Apple, IKEA); KISS principle history; Miller's Architecture of Simplicity; complexity costs research; competitive repertoire dynamics; the simplicity-performance tradeoff; business model complexity and imitation barriers; innovation strategy complexity; user-reported experiences.
Out: UI/UX design simplicity; life philosophy minimalism; software KISS at code level.
Timeframe: 1960-2026. Geography: Global.

## Dimensions
Technical: Measuring strategic simplicity (competitive repertoire index, complexity cost metrics)
Historical: KISS principle, Maeda's Laws, Miller's Architecture, Dieter Rams
State-of-art: Current academic consensus, MIT complexity sweet spot, digitization effects
Quantitative: All empirical studies with effect sizes (Miller, Ferrier, Lumpkin, Connelly, Tavassoli, Rivkin)
Stakeholders: CEOs, consultants, employees, customers, investors
Competing: Complexify school (Tsoukas), RBV, imitation barriers, product proliferation strategies
Criticisms: Simplicity as rigidity, imitation vulnerability, over-simplification danger, industry contingency
Contrarian: Complexity as competitive advantage, Amazon's complexity, complex strategies harder to copy
Future: AI effect on simplicity, generative strategy, one-page strategy dominance
Regulatory: ESG complexity penalty, compliance-driven complexity costs
Geographic: US, Europe, South Africa, Asia differences
Practical: How to simplify, one-page strategy, stop-doing lists
Public opinion: Reddit, HN, LinkedIn practitioner perspectives

## Sources
Min 216 sources (target). 4+ source types. Mix recent & foundational. Both sides cited. Geographic diversity.

## Output
Extremely concise language. Executive summary (800-1500w). 6-8 findings. Synthesis. Limitations. Recommendations. Full bibliography. Methodology appendix. ELI5 explanations.
