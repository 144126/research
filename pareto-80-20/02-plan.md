## Triangulation Approach
- Every major claim: 3+ independent sources
- Claims labeled by evidence class (expert/third-party, user-reported, vendor-sourced)
- Contradictions explicitly flagged and discussed
- Single-source claims marked as weak evidence

## Source Type Targets
- Academic papers: 80+
- Books: 30+
- Wikipedia/technical docs: 20+
- News/popular articles: 40+
- Forum/community: 60+
- Primary sources: 10+
- Statistician blogs: 20+
- Geographic diversity: studies from US, Europe, China, developing nations

## Quality Gates
- 270+ sources with avg credibility > 75/100
- Every claim cited in same sentence [N]
- No placeholder text anywhere
- Bibliography complete with all citations
