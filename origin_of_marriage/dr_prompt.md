# Deep Research Prompt: Origin of Marriage

## Research Question
What is the exact, true origin of marriage as a human institution — cutting through mythology, religious tradition, and cultural bias to deliver the definitive evidence-based account of where, when, why, and how marriage first emerged?

## Purpose
Settle the question of whether marriage is a "natural" human institution with deep evolutionary roots or a relatively recent cultural invention tied to agriculture and property.

## Audience
A 54-year-old academic-minded man AND his 9-year-old child. Use inline [imagine: ...] explanations for every technical term, ancient name, or abstract concept.

## Required Dimensions

2. Hunter-Gatherer Ancestral Reconstruction — Walker et al. 2011 PLOS ONE, 85% arranged marriage, 80% brideprice/brideservice

7. Legal Evolution — Roman law affectio maritalis, English common law coverture
8. Romantic Revolution — Stephanie Coontz, 19th century origins
